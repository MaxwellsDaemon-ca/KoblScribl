These thoughts of more were a dangerous and treacherous thing, a path she knew she shouldn’t wander down. She had never wanted to. Why should it be any different this time? It was a rare night, a perfect night, a wild and riveting night with a client like no other. Would she not cheapen it by trying to make it more?

Her breath hitched again as she snuggled into Novu’s side, as she tried and failed to let go of the irrational parts of her that longed for something deeper, something lasting.

“But,” the Suicune began again, her voice edged with something almost like shyness. Dazzle blinked, her attention zeroing in on Novu as she continued, “If you would like to meet again… outside the club… I’d love to get to know you better.” Was she serious? Was she really inviting her to something more?

For a moment, Dazzle felt like she was adrift in the confusion again, but this time, the feeling was lighter, buoyed by excitement. She couldn’t quite believe that Novu wanted her like this, not just in a consuming night of passion but in the world beyond. Not able to form any words, Dazzle just nodded, her heart a whirlwind of hope and disbelief. She wrapped her flippers around Novu’s midsection and cuddled in, rubbing her face against the barrel chest of the Suicune as though she could make sense of it all just by being closer.

Perhaps… perhaps getting to know one of her clients wouldn’t be so bad.
