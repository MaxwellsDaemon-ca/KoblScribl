Alexei's eyes cracked open despite the crust sealing them shut. His alarm was buzzing and the crack from his nightstand drawer-slash-bed showed it was still pre-daylight hours. 

He reached around blindly for the conn-orb, finding it after a few misses. He pulled it open to turn off the alarm. 6:01 AM. *Riiiight. Work. That's a thing.*

The salandit was sure that he would have gotten at least some sleep last night if it wasn't for the fact that someone in the apartment block was yowling like a cat in heat. He didn't want to point digits, but the only feline pokemon that lived here – that he was aware of at least – was old granny Merryweather, the glameow on the third floor. 

*Good on you granny, but aren't old ladies like you supposed to be asleep that late at night?*

He at least got a few hours of sleep, so that was good. He could work with that. 

Pulling the drawer open and slipping out, he went about the usual morning routine before he had to head to the library. Slipping into the shower he gave himself a quick scrub and brushed his teeth while letting the scalding water (for non-fire types, at least) rush over him. The water pressure may have been shit in the apartment but at least the water heater worked. 

He glanced at the clock on the wall after he got out of the bathroom, noting that he had an hour and a half before work left. An hour of that would be the walk – he enjoyed walking to work, and it kept him fairly in shape despite his mainly sedentary lifestyle. The library was about 12 blocks away from his place, and he liked to give himself a bit of extra time in case he had to reroute.

His stomach grumbled at him as he was going to sit down to enjoy a small conn-orb doomscroll, and he realized that due to... everything that happened yesterday, he never grabbed lunch or dinner. *Tali'd be on my ass to eat, heh, she's like a mother Kangaskhan.*

The thought stung more than it should have. 

Right! Food, then work. 

Grabbing his satchel, keys and some extra poké for a decent-sized breakfast, he headed out. Thankfully there was a decent breakfast café on the way.
